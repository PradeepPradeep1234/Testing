Technical Core

Inside the Reactor Engine

Understanding the science behind our chemical-free water treatment process.

01

IONIC DISRUPTION

Targets Calcium ions using high-frequency electrolytic pulses generated by MMO coated titanium electrodes. This disrupts the mineral's crystalline structure at the ionic level.

02

CRYSTAL SHIFT

Converts sticky calcite (Calcium Carbonate) into non-adherent Aragonite crystals that flow freely through pipes without forming scale deposits on surfaces.

03

TITANIUM CORE

Medical-grade MMO coated Titanium Anodes ensure zero corrosion and 100% operational efficiency, providing a long service life with minimal maintenance requirements.

B Soft Installation Guide

Recommended standards for effective scale prevention and system safety.

🔧 Plumbing Instructions

1
Source

Water drawn from Borewell using pump.

2
Pre-Filtration

Pump outlet connected to Pre Bag Filter.

3
Control

Filtered water flows through Flow Switch.

4
Core System

Flow switch outlet connected to B soft unit.

5
Polishing

B soft outlet connected to Post Bag Filter.

6
Storage & Use

Water delivered to Overhead Tank for household distribution.

Electrical Installation

  • Connect to a stabilized 230V AC power source.
  • Ensure the Flow Switch is interlocked with the B soft unit.
  • Use waterproof conduits for all outdoor wiring.
Standard Installation Requirements
  • ✓ Minimum 1" inlet/outlet pipe size
  • ✓ Maximum inlet pressure: 8 bar
  • ✓ Temperature: 5°C to 60°C operating range
  • ✓ TDS input: < 2000 ppm recommended

Advanced Science

Revolutionary REDOX Technology

A permanent, chemical-free solution to water hardness through advanced electrochemical mineral conversion.

99% Scale Reduction Guaranteed
1
Electrolysis Process

MMO coated titanium electrodes create a controlled electrolysis environment that targets hard water minerals at the ionic level.

2
Ion Conversion

The REDOX process converts divalent calcium (Ca²⁺) and magnesium (Mg²⁺) ions into monovalent ions that don't form scale.

3
Permanent Softening

Unlike ion exchange, our process permanently alters the mineral structure, preventing scale formation on all surfaces.

4
Less Monitoring

A long-lasting system designed for continuous service with significantly less monitoring than traditional chemical plants.
