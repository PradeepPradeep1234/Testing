Advanced Science

Revolutionary REDOX Technology

A permanent, chemical-free solution to water hardness through advanced electrochemical mineral conversion.

99% Scale Reduction
1

Electrolysis Process

Our MMO coated titanium electrodes create a controlled electrolysis environment that targets hard water minerals.

2

Ion Conversion

The REDOX process converts divalent calcium and magnesium ions ($Ca^{2+}$, $Mg^{2+}$) to monovalent ions that don't form scale.

3

Permanent Softening

Unlike traditional softeners that use ion exchange, our process permanently alters the mineral structure, preventing scale formation.

4

Less Monitoring

A long-lasting system designed for continuous service with significantly less monitoring than traditional chemical plants.
